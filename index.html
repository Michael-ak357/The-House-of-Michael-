Welcome

Welcome to the House of Michael. Our mission is to prepare the people for the second coming of the Messiah. This is done through comparing scriptures with scriptures, to weed out unauthorized scriptures implemented by the church intothe Bible. We built spiritual understanding, encourage faith, and build a community centered on the Holy Spirit, and His son Jesus Christ.

Our Beliefs

We seek out the truth by comparing scriptures with scriptures, for three or more scriptures will confirm one another. With this process, we can walk in the foundation of truth that God intended. The correct word provides guidance, wisdom, and the way into eternal life.

Our teachings focus on faith, repentance, obedience to God, and the message of the Holy Spirit through Jesus Christ.
